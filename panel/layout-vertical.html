How You Can Register your Brand

CoShop Brand Registry helps protect your intellectual property and guard your brand’s good name on Coshop.

CoShop Brand Registry helps protect your intellectual property and guard your brand’s good name on Coshop.

Adding your company to Brand Registry is also how you get access to all of the free tools, reports, and benefits that come with selling your brand in the Coshop store.

To enroll your business in Coshop Brand Registry, you’ll need:
•	An Coshop seller account (sign up now).
•	An active registered trademark in the country where you want to enroll, or a pending trademark application filed through Coshop IP Accelerator.
•	Your brand name or logo displayed prominently on your products and packaging.
If you meet the criteria, here’s how to sign up for Coshop Brand Registry:
1.	Go to  brandservices.coshop.com/eligibility and click “Enroll now.”
2.	Choose the country-specific marketplace in which you want to enroll your brand.
3.	Follow the prompt to log in to Seller Central.
4.	Complete the brief form by providing information about your business as it appears on legal documents or trademark applications.
5.	After you submit the form, you’ll be directed to the Brand Registry page connected to your seller account. Select Manage >> Enroll a brand
6.	Follow the steps to submit your enrollment for review.
The information you provide during enrollment will depend on the country-specific marketplace where you want to enroll. In the India, for example, CoShop Brand Registry requests:
•	Trademark registration number with the Indian Patent Trademark office.
•	 Your “brand marks,” which could be words/text or an images/designs (such as a logo).
•	Product categories in which your brand products should be listed (such as apparel, sporting goods, electronics, for example).
•	Countries where your brand’s products are manufactured and distributed.
Customize product detail pages with A+ Content
Once your brand is approved for CoShop Brand Registry, it’s time for the fun stuff. Brand Registry unlocks free tools that help you manage your brand products behind the scenes and manage your brand story as it appears for customers shopping the CoShop store.

With A+ Content, you can build a beautiful brand experience on CoShop. A+ Content enhances your product listings, and Stores allows you to create a multi-page shopping experience within the CoShop store.


WHAT NEXT:
Once you build an online store within the CoShop store, take advantage of the many other ecommerce marketing and online seller tools we’ve created to help brands reach more customers.

Drive traffic to your CoShop store and product listings using Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Products, and other CoShop Advertising strategies. Use your unique CoShop URL to marketing campaigns on and off CoShop.

Use free tools in Seller Central to run A/B tests on your product detail page content, host shoppable livestream events, sell virtual bundles, and more.
